Our Group Reflection

Role that ONA Plays in the Cultural Heritage of the Philippines

- Emerging artists from the Philippines plays a significant role in the Philippine's cultural heritage. This is where ONA comes to play as its main purpose is to promote and encourage them. ONA or Order of the National Artist is the most Honorable Award an Artist can receive, it embodies the nation's ideals in humanism and aesthetic expression through Architecture, Literature Dance, Theater and etc. The promotion of art in a nation plays an important role in the cultural heritage of one's nation in this case the Philippines.
